Paul M. Haderlie, DVM

Paul Haderlie graduated from Brigham Young University with a degree in Animal Science. He received his veterinary degree at Colorado State University and then completed an equine medicine and surgery internship at Littleton Large Animal Clinic in Colorado. He began private practice in Utah County in 1989. In 1992 he opened Rocky Mountain Large Animal Clinic in Pleasant Grove, Utah with Dr. Bruce Lee. Following Dr. Lee’s tragic death in an automobile accident in 1997, Dr. Haderlie built his present clinic in Spanish Fork. He has enjoyed the varied aspects of equine medicine and surgery, and his association with so many great horse people.

Dr. Haderlie enjoys working with his wife Leslie, who manages the office. Together they have 4 children and 11 grandchildren. In his free time, Dr. Haderlie enjoys spending time with his family and friends, riding his favorite saddle mule, and hunting.
